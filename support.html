Support

Want to support the funding of Catalyst?

The Catalyst Team is here to serve, but we also need funding in order to continue providing services to the community. Right now, all Catalyst efforts are self funded, and the Catalyst team wants to invite you to partnering with us financially. We are grateful for whatever you choose to give. Join us in our mission to connect Christian professionals and unify God's expanding kingdom!

Have questions about being a sponsor? Send us an email to learn how you can support the Catalyst Team as a church, corporate or university sponsor.

Looking to help out in another way?

We are always looking for passionate people to join in and help out. If you have an area you can contribute to (eg. social media, admin work, developing, designing), shoot us an email and lets chat!
