FAQ

How does Catalyst work?

At the beginning of each week, we send you an email asking if you're available for a meeting Thursday, Friday or Saturday. If you are, we will work on matching you with someone in your area.

Once we find you a match, you'll recieve an email (Wednesdsay at latest) with your meeting details-who your match is, where to meet and at what time.

After the meeting, we want to hear how it went, so expect an email asking for your feedback. This will give us more insight into better meeting planning and matching.

Do I have to go to a meeting each week?

Nope! At the start of each week, you can simply say you don't want a meeting, or just don't reply!

We kindly ask that you only say you're available if you are actually available. If you are unsure, it is okay to just wait until the next week. We ask for you to be committed to meeting so your meeting partner will have a great experience.

If you happen to cancel multiple accounts or have low-quality meetings we will have to suspend your account. We care about our users and how their meeting experiences go. Let's work together to provide a great experience.

Do I get to see who I am matched up with before I accept?

Unfortunaely you don't. We want meetings to be convenient and for people to meet others they usually wouldn't. Our job is to connect, so your meetings are our priority.

What if I can't meet during any of the time choices?

We are constantly working through our system to make the service best for you. We suggest you sign up and we will notify you when more times open.

Does Catalyst cost?

Nope! The service is free, enjoy.

What if something comes up last minute and I can't make it?

You will be in contact with your match, so just notify them. We suggest you share phone numbers to ease the communication through email.

If you continue to miss meeting after meeting, you should reprioritze and only commit to finding a match if you know you can make it.

What should I expect from a Catalyst meeting?

Connecting you to Christian professionals is our goal. We want to connect people outside of their immidate circles, furthering the unity of the body of Christ. New connections will bring life, new opportunity and a chance to love others.

What if I'm running late to a Catalyst meetings?

When we match you up, we suggest sharing your phone number so you can easily communicate with your match. People have busy schedules, let's be respectful of them and stay within the prefixed time.

How long does a Catalyst meeting last?

As long as you want! We suggest 30 minutes, but communicate with your match and don't hesitate to let them know how long you can meet for. We will give you the time and place to meet, the rest is up to you.

No longer want to participate?

Just shoot us an email at help@catalyst.com
